ABOUT

US

Al Taqwa Foundation was established with the vision of serving humanity and supporting those in need. Guided by the values of compassion, unity, and responsibility, our foundation works to provide assistance in education, food relief, healthcare support, and community development.

Together for Humanity

Our Mission
& Vision

Mission

To serve humanity through charitable initiatives by supporting the needy, promoting education, encouraging social responsibility, and spreading kindness and unity in the community.

Vision

To create a compassionate and empowered community where everyone has access to basic needs, education, and opportunities for a better future.
